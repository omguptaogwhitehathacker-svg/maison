Our story

Single restaurant

Maison Agra began as a single table on a quiet rooftop in Taj Ganj. Today, it remains intentionally small — twenty-four seats, one open kitchen, and a menu shaped by what the morning market brings in.

We cook the food we love: blistered Neapolitan pizzas, hand-rolled pasta, and the slow, smoky mughlai classics that define this city. Nothing more, nothing less.
